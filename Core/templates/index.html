Pharmosome

Database for SNP analysis and Explorer

Pharmosome Data Set

Containf massive information about all available SNPs and its relationship with disease development and drug responce including the pathway of disease and the gene annotation with linking of eight database

1.

SNP

SNP sub-database contain the clinical annotation of SNP and the annotation of gene in which SNP located and its pathway which involved in disease pathogenesis (if available) with in detail description of gene transcript and its different splicing variants

2.

Chemical

Chemical sub-database contain all available chemical involved in disease treatment

3.

Disease

Disease sub-database contain information about disease the clinical annotation that describe how different SNP can alter drug responce and metabolization

4.

Gene

Gene sub-database collects almost all genes either involved in disease pathogenesis or not with detailed annotation of each gene collected from different databases

5.

SNP Collector

SNP Collector can be used to collect the SNPs located on gene, chromosome, related to disease, related to chemical

6.

SNP Phylogeney

SNP Phylogeney linking between different SNP by aligning the sequence of each SNP to illustrate how far SNPs are related to each others

7.

Pick Primer

Pick Primer tool provide Reverse primer, foreword primer and probe to maximize the ability to catch the SNP from the large genome
